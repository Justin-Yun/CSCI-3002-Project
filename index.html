About:

Money, a piece of paper that enables economies to exist. People use it everyday, but for some, they are not aware about how much money they are spending. When people go out to shop, pay bills, and fulfill their basic needs, they tend to lose track of their spending. So, we wanted to create an app to help with that.

We wanted an application that helps track peoples' spendings and offer tips on saving money. We also wanted some type of real time tracking; therefore, all the data within the application would be instant.

Features:

We wanted to implement features that were going to benefit the user's daily life. Here are some of the functions provided within the app.

Alerts

Notifies user with about daily changes within their finances.

Savings

Keeps track of their savings and their other accounts.

Spending

Tracks the user's daily spending and tracks their spending habits.

Suggestions

Provides suggestions and tips on budgeting and saving money.
